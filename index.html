About me

I am currently a PhD student of Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science (AMSS), Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS), under the supervision of Prof. Yu-Hong Dai. Before that, I received the Bachelor degree in mathematics and applied mathematics at University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS). My research interests include integer programming, optimization in power system and learning to optimize.

Research Interests

  • Integer Programming

  • Optimization in Power System

  • Learning to Optimize
